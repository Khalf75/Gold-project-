Welcome, To Gold world

Gold has been treasured since ancient times, used in jewelry, currency, and art. It fueled exploration, sparked gold rushes, and remains a symbol of wealth and prestige.

Companies

Thani Stratex Resources

Thani Stratex Resources has been involved in gold exploration activities in Egypt, particularly in the Eastern Desert region.

Centamin Egypt Limited

Centamin operates the Sukari Gold Mine, which is Egypt's largest and oldest gold mine. It is located in the Eastern Desert, approximately 700 kilometers from Cairo.

Aton Resources Inc

Aton Resources is involved in exploration and development projects in Egypt, with a focus on the Arabian-Nubian Shield. They have projects such as the Abu Marawat Concession.

AL-AMAR INTERNATIONAL MINING COMPANY

Al-Amar International Mining Company is involved in mining and exploration activities, including gold, in Egypt.

Cyprium Mining Corporation

Cyprium Mining Corporation has explored gold and other minerals in various locations in Egypt.

ALEXANDRIA MINERAL OIL COMPANY (AMOC)

While primarily associated with the oil and gas sector, AMOC has also been involved in exploration activities for various minerals, including gold.

Countries

Australia

Australia is a significant contributor to the global gold market.

United States

The United States has historically been a major player in the gold market.

Russia
